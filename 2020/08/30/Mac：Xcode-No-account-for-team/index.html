Mac：Xcode No account for team
发表于2020-08-30|更新于2020-10-17|Mac
|字数总计:88|阅读时长:1分钟|阅读量:

问题

Xcode报错误信息：
error: No account for team “PPW9FUQJ5U”. Add a new account in the Accounts preference pane or verify that your accounts have valid credentials. (in target ‘KCObjc’ from project ‘objc’)
error: No signing certificate “Mac Development” found: No “Mac Development” signing certificate matching team ID “PPW9FUQJ5U” with a private key was found. (in target ‘KCObjc’ from project ‘objc’)

原因：项目中的teamID未修改。

解决

搜索 PPW9FUQJ5U 并修改为自己的ID。
